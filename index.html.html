WHO Disability Classification

Step 1 — Monaural Impairment (per ear)

If PTA ≤ 25 dB HL → MI = 0% If PTA > 25 dB HL → MI = (PTA − 25) × 1.5%

Step 2 — Binaural / Overall Hearing Disability

Disability = (5 × Better_Ear_MI + 1 × Poorer_Ear_MI) / 6

The better ear is weighted 5× more because binaural hearing performance is dominated by the better-functioning ear.

All PTA values must be within 0 – 120 dB HL. Final result is capped at 100%.

Reference: American Academy of Otolaryngology (AAO-HNS) · Indian Disability Evaluation & Assessment Scale (IDEAS)
